# Risk Reversal Evaluation Rubric

## Purpose
Evaluate risk reversal and anxiety reduction for conversion effectiveness.

## Types of Risk Reversal

1. **Money-Back Guarantee** - Full refund if not satisfied
2. **Free Trial** - Try before buying
3. **No Credit Card Required** - Zero commitment to try
4. **Cancel Anytime** - No lock-in or penalties
5. **Specific Timeframe** - "30-day money back"
6. **Results Guarantee** - Tied to specific outcome
7. **Unconditional Guarantee** - No questions asked

## Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Guarantee Strength (Weight: 35%)
- Is the guarantee bold and confident?
- Is it specific (not vague conditions)?
- Would it meaningfully reduce risk?
- Is it unconditional or conditional?

**Strength Spectrum:**
- WEAK: "Satisfaction guaranteed (terms apply)"
- MODERATE: "30-day money-back guarantee"
- STRONG: "100% money back if you don't see results in 60 days. No questions asked."

**Red Flags:**
- Vague guarantees with hidden conditions
- Short timeframes that don't allow real testing
- Buried in fine print
- Too many conditions to feel safe

### 2. Visibility and Prominence (Weight: 30%)
- Is risk reversal visible near CTAs?
- Is it mentioned early enough to reduce anxiety?
- Is it repeated at key decision points?
- Is it visually distinct (badge, icon)?

**Red Flags:**
- Only in footer or terms page
- Not near any CTA
- No visual emphasis
- Easily missed when scanning

### 3. Completeness (Weight: 20%)
- Are all major anxieties addressed?
- Is payment security addressed?
- Is commitment level clear?
- Is the process for refund/cancellation clear?

**Anxiety Checklist:**
- [ ] Money risk (refund/guarantee)
- [ ] Time risk (cancel anytime)
- [ ] Commitment risk (no credit card/free trial)
- [ ] Privacy risk (we don't share/sell data)
- [ ] Security risk (SSL, payment badges)

**Red Flags:**
- Major anxiety type not addressed
- Process unclear or sounds difficult
- Missing security assurance for payment

### 4. Specificity (Weight: 15%)
- Is timeframe for guarantee specific?
- Is the refund process explained?
- Are conditions (if any) clear?

**Red Flags:**
- "Money back guarantee" without timeframe
- No explanation of how to get refund
- Hidden conditions in fine print

## Risk Reversal Placement

| Location | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Near CTA | Reduce click anxiety |
| Pricing section | Justify purchase |
| Checkout | Prevent abandonment |
| Hero area | Early anxiety reduction |
| FAQ | Detailed explanation |

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 9-10  | Bold guarantee, prominently placed, all anxieties addressed |
| 7-8   | Good guarantee visible at key points, minor gaps |
| 5-6   | Present but weak, buried, or incomplete coverage |
| 3-4   | Minimal or vague guarantee, poor placement |
| 1-2   | No meaningful risk reversal present |
