# Rubric: Landing Page Audit

Comprehensive evaluation framework for landing page effectiveness.

## Scoring

### 5 - Exceptional
- Immediately clear value proposition in hero
- Single, compelling CTA that stands out
- Strong social proof with specific, believable results
- Perfect message match with traffic source
- Mobile-optimized with fast load time
- Every element supports the conversion goal

### 4 - Strong
- Clear value proposition, minor refinements possible
- CTA is prominent and action-oriented
- Good social proof, could be more specific
- Message aligns well with traffic source
- Minor mobile or speed issues
- Most elements support conversion

### 3 - Adequate
- Value proposition present but not compelling
- CTA exists but doesn't stand out
- Social proof present but generic
- Some message mismatch with traffic source
- Noticeable mobile or performance issues
- Competing elements or distractions present

### 2 - Weak
- Unclear value proposition, requires scrolling
- Multiple CTAs competing for attention
- Weak or missing social proof
- Significant message mismatch
- Poor mobile experience
- Navigation or links leading away

### 1 - Slop
- No clear value proposition
- Generic or confusing CTAs ("Submit", "Learn More")
- No social proof or fake testimonials
- Complete disconnect from traffic source
- Broken on mobile
- More about the company than the visitor

## Red Flags (Instant Slop Indicators)
- Navigation bar on a focused landing page
- "Welcome to [Company Name]" as headline
- Stock photos of handshakes or office workers
- "Submit" button text
- Walls of text without visual hierarchy
- Auto-playing video or audio
- Slider/carousel in hero section
- Multiple form submissions required
- No mobile optimization
- Slow load time (>3 seconds)

## Evaluation Questions
1. Can I understand who this is for and what I get in 5 seconds?
2. Is there exactly one thing I'm supposed to do?
3. Would I believe these testimonials if I were skeptical?
4. Does the headline match what I clicked to get here?
5. Can I complete the goal on my phone?
6. What would stop me from converting right now?
7. Is every element earning its place on the page?
