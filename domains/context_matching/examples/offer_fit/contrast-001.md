Awareness level: Problem Aware

## Teaching Point
A lead magnet must feel like a gift, not a transaction. The visitor should think "I can't believe this is free" not "I guess I'll give my email for this."

## When to Apply
Before creating any lead capture, answer: "Would I give my email for this from a stranger?" If no, make the offer more valuable or the ask smaller.
